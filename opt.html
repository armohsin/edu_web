Internships

Participating in an internship allows you to gain a better perspective of post-graduation employment by applying the principles and theories you've developed in the classroom. This practical application creates an easier transition for you from the classroom to the working world. You will further develop a personal work ethic and be able to investigate your career interests and prospective career goals. Additionally, an internship enables you to develop specific skills and knowledge related to your potential career.
